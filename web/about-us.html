About AC Techs

Last updated: 2026-06-12

Application Brand: AC Techs
Engineering & Delivery: Aeonis Labs

AC Techs is an internal operations app for AC installation technicians and administrators. It helps the team capture jobs, manage approvals, track earnings and expenses, and keep audit-ready records across web and Android.

What the app does

  • Technicians submit daily job records, in/out entries, and support notes
  • Administrators review approvals, analytics, settlements, and team records
  • Release builds include crash reporting for reliability monitoring
