AC Techs Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-11

Data we collect

  • Account profile data (name, email, role, optional phone)
  • Operational data for jobs, approvals, earnings, and expenses
  • Technical crash diagnostics from Firebase Crashlytics (release builds)

How we use data

  • Provide technician and admin workflows in AC Techs
  • Maintain audit trails and approval history
  • Improve app reliability by investigating crashes

Data sharing

We do not sell personal data. Data is processed only for internal business operations and is stored in project-controlled Firebase services.

Security

Access is controlled with Firebase Authentication and Firestore security rules. Role-based permissions are enforced server-side.

Contact

For policy questions, contact your AC Techs administrator.
